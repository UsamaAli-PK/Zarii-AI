# ZARii AI: The Smart Brain of Pakistan's Agriculture

ZARii AI is an easy-to-use platform that helps Pakistani farmers grow better crops and earn more money. We use smart technology to give farmers the expert advice they need, right on their phones.

---

## 🚀 Working Features (Live Now)

### 1. 🎙️ Urdu Voice Assistant (Speak and Listen)
Farmers don't need to type. They can just **speak in Urdu** to ask about crop diseases, weather, or sprays. The app will speak back the answer in a clear Urdu voice.

### 2. 📸 Instant Photo Diagnosis (Vision AI)
Take a photo of a sick plant leaf. ZARii AI will:
- Tell you the **disease name** in Urdu and English.
- Suggest the **right spray or fertilizer** to fix it.
- Show you the **current price** in Pakistan so you don't get overcharged.

### 3. 🚨 Local Disease Alerts (Early Warning)
The system watches your district. If many neighbors are seeing a specific disease (like Wheat Rust), ZARii AI will send an **Alert** to your phone so you can protect your crop before it's too late.

### 4. 🌦️ Smart Weather Advice
ZARii AI looks at the humidity and heat in your specific tehsil. If the weather is likely to cause a disease, it warns you: *"It's very humid today. Spray now to prevent fungus."*

### 5. 🤝 Trusted Product Suggestions
When a disease is found, the app shows you the best-selling products from trusted companies. You can see which products are most effective in your specific area.

### 6. 📱 WhatsApp Support
Farmers get their results sent directly to their WhatsApp. If the AI is unsure, the photo is automatically sent to a **Real Agronomist** (Human Expert) for a final check.

## 💎 Future Roadmap (Coming Soon)

### 🏦 Loan & Insurance Support (Agri-Fintech)
We are building a system where farmers with a good history of using ZARii AI can more easily get **Bank Loans** or **Crop Insurance** because they have proof of their hard work.

### 🌍 Export Quality Checker
Help for farmers who want to sell to Dubai, Europe, or the USA. The app will warn you if you are using a spray that is banned in those countries, ensuring your crop is safe to export.

### 📊 National Health Map
A live map for the government and big companies to see exactly where diseases are spreading across Pakistan, helping everyone respond faster.
