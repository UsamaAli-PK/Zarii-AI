## Summary
The primary entry point and high-level overview of the ZARii AI project. It describes ZARii AI as a bilingual AI-powered crop disease diagnosis platform for Pakistani farmers.

## Key Takeaways
- **Mission**: Help Pakistani farmers grow better crops and earn more money through expert advice via mobile phones.
- **Core Features**: Urdu Voice Assistant, Instant Photo Diagnosis, Local Disease Alerts, Smart Weather Advice, Trusted Product Suggestions, and WhatsApp Support.
- **Technology**: Node.js, Supabase, Google Gemini, OpenAI, ElevenLabs.
- **Roadmap**: Agri-Fintech (Agri-Credit Score), Export Quality Checker, National Health Map.
