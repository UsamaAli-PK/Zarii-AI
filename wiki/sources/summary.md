## Summary
A high-level executive summary of ZARii AI, highlighting live features and the future roadmap.

## Key Takeaways
- **Bilingual Interface**: Focus on Urdu Voice Assistant to remove literacy barriers.
- **Vision AI**: Photos of plants for diagnosis and treatment suggestions.
- **Early Warning System**: District-level disease alerts based on neighbor scan density.
- **Smart Weather**: Weather-disease correlation (humidity/heat) for preventative advice.
- **Human Fallback**: WhatsApp integration for expert agronomist review.
- **Future Growth**: Loan/Insurance (Agri-Fintech), Export Quality Checker, National Health Map.
