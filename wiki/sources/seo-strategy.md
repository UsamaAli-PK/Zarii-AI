# Source: zarii-seo-strategy.md

## Summary
A comprehensive strategy for SEO, GEO, and Programmatic SEO (pSEO) to ensure ZARii AI is the authority on Pakistani agriculture.

## Key Takeaways
- **Technical SEO**: Transitioned from invisible SPA to crawlable SSR using Express.
- **Programmatic SEO (pSEO)**: Scaling to 35+ pages (diseases, pesticides, regions, glossary) using live DB data.
- **GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)**: Optimizing for AI citation (ChatGPT, Perplexity) via statistics, expert citations, and "answer-first" structure.
- **Information Gain Moat**: Using proprietary data (outbreak counts, real PKR prices) that competitors don't have.
- **Playbooks**:
    1. Disease Guides (`/diseases/`)
    2. Pesticide Prices (`/pesticides/`)
    3. Region Hubs (`/farmers/`)
    4. Glossary (`/learn/`)
