# ZARii AI

The core product and organization. A bilingual AI-powered agricultural assistant platform focused on the Pakistani market.

## Overview
ZARii AI addresses the knowledge gap for farmers in Pakistan by providing localized, high-tech agricultural advice in Urdu and English. It leverages modern AI (Vision, NLP, TTS) to diagnose crop diseases from photos and provide voice-based guidance.

## Key Features
- **Vision AI Diagnosis**: Identifying diseases from leaf photos.
- **Urdu Voice interaction**: Using [[openai]] Whisper for STT and ElevenLabs for TTS.
- **Early Warning System**: Monitoring regional outbreaks and weather patterns.

## Tech Stack
Built with Node.js, [[supabase]], [[google-gemini]], and OpenAI.

## Roadmap
Evolving into a "Farmer OS" with [[agri-fintech]] and export compliance features.
