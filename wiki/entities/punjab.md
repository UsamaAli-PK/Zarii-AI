# Punjab

Pakistan's primary agricultural province and the main market for [[zarii-ai]]'s crop disease diagnosis platform.

## Agricultural Profile

Punjab is Pakistan's agricultural heartland:

- **Area:** 205,344 km² (largest province by area)
- **Population:** ~130 million (largest by population)
- **Arable Land:** ~80% of Pakistan's cultivated area
- **Irrigation:** Extensive canal system (Indus Basin)
- **Climate:** Semi-arid to subtropical; hot summers, mild winters

## Major Crops

- **Wheat:** Winter crop (October-April); staple grain
- **Cotton:** Summer crop (May-September); cash crop
- **Rice:** Summer crop; grown in southern Punjab
- **Sugarcane:** Year-round; major cash crop
- **Maize:** Summer crop; increasing adoption
- **Vegetables:** Year-round; high-value crops

## Disease Patterns

Punjab's warm, humid climate creates ideal conditions for:

- **Fungal Diseases:** Powdery mildew, leaf rust, early blight
- **Bacterial Diseases:** Bacterial leaf blight, bacterial wilt
- **Viral Diseases:** Mosaic viruses, leaf curl viruses
- **Pest Pressure:** High insect populations (whitefly, aphids)

Disease severity varies by season:

- **Monsoon (July-September):** Peak fungal disease pressure
- **Winter (December-February):** Moderate disease pressure
- **Summer (May-June):** High pest pressure

## Farmer Demographics

- **Total Farmers:** ~5 million
- **Farm Size:** Average 2-5 acres (small to medium)
- **Literacy:** ~45% agricultural literacy
- **Technology Adoption:** Growing smartphone penetration (60%+)
- **Language:** Urdu, Punjabi, English

## Economic Impact

- **Agricultural GDP:** ~25% of Punjab's economy
- **Employment:** ~40% of rural population
- **Exports:** Cotton, rice, wheat to global markets
- **Challenges:** Water scarcity, soil degradation, climate variability

## Zarii-AI Opportunity

Punjab represents the largest market opportunity:

- Highest farmer concentration
- Most developed agricultural infrastructure
- Strongest smartphone penetration
- Highest willingness to adopt technology
- Largest potential revenue
