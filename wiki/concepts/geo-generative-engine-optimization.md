# GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)

The strategy of optimizing content to be cited by Generative AI engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews.

## Implementation in ZARii AI
- **Statistics with Specifics**: Every page includes a "Stat Box" with hard numbers (yield loss %, economic thresholds).
- **Expert Citations**: Referencing official bodies like PCCC, NARC, and PARC to build authority.
- **Answer-First Structure**: Opening each SEO page with a 40-60 word direct answer block.
- **Entity Clarity**: Explicitly naming diseases, pathogens, locations, and brands to maximize entity matching in AI knowledge graphs.

## Research Base
Based on research from Princeton and IIT Delhi (KDD 2024) showing that structured writing and specific statistics provide the highest lift in AI visibility.
