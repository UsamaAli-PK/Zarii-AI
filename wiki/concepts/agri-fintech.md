# Agri-Fintech

A core component of the [[zarii-ai]] roadmap, focusing on financial inclusion for Pakistani farmers.

## Vision
Transform agricultural data into financial opportunity by leveraging farmer activity and crop health history.

## Key Initiatives
- **Agri-Credit Score**: Building a creditworthiness profile based on consistent platform usage, successful disease management, and yield history.
- **Loan & Insurance Support**: Partnering with banks and insurance companies to provide easier access to capital and crop protection.
- **Proof of Work**: Using ZARii AI records as verifiable proof of farming activity for financial institutions.
